Diabetic Diet and Health Recommendations

As a diabetic, maintaining a healthy diet is crucial. Here are some tips:

Focus on whole grains, vegetables, and lean proteins.

Limit sugary foods and drinks.

Include high-fiber foods like legumes, nuts, and seeds.

Eat smaller meals throughout the day to regulate blood sugar levels.

Recommended Foods for Diabetics:

Leafy greens (spinach, kale, etc.)

Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa, etc.)

Lean meats (chicken, turkey, fish)

Legumes (lentils, chickpeas, beans)

Low-fat dairy products (yogurt, cheese, etc.)
