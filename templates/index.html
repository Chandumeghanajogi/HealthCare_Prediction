What is Healthcare Prediction for Diabetic Patients?

This tool uses a machine learning model to predict whether a person is diabetic or non-diabetic based on various health metrics. The model uses several key indicators such as glucose levels, insulin levels, BMI, age, and more to assess the likelihood of diabetes.

Factors that affect the prediction:

  • Pregnancies: The number of pregnancies a person has had.
  • Glucose: Glucose level in the blood.
  • Blood Pressure: The person's blood pressure levels.
  • Skin Thickness: Skin thickness measured at the triceps.
  • Insulin: Insulin level in the blood.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (a measure of body fat based on height and weight).
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A measure of genetic predisposition to diabetes.
  • Age: The person's age.
